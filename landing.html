Why I Built VulnLens

I am a Computer Science student at Hochschule Darmstadt with a strong interest in both AI and cybersecurity. I built VulnLens to explore how AI can make vulnerability intelligence more accessible, structured, and actionable for developers who do not have access to expensive security tooling.

✓ Built on real NVD vulnerability data
✓ Combines RAG, vector search, and LLM reasoning
